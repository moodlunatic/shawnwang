My Journey of Data Science



Uncovering the network

The Securities and Exchange Commission's (SEC) Form 13F is a quarterly report that is required to be filed by all institutional investment managers with at least $100 million in assets under management in American.

With the aim of finding the relationships between different financial firms , I used Python and networkx to perform web scraping on SEC website, build up network of investment and conduct data analysis on the network.
